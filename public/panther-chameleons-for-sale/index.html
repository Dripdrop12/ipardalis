Ambilobe Panther Chameleons for sale

Our chameleons are all available for purchase at varying prices based on their age, gender, lineage, and specific coloration. The Ambilobe chameleons for sale here come from a variety of different captive bred bloodlines, resulting in unique and beautiful color patterns. Whether you're looking for a vibrant Yellow Body Blue Bar (YBBB) or a striking Red Body Blue Bar (RBBB), there is a chameleon here to suit you. Each chameleon is carefully bred and raised in an individual enclosure to ensure they are healthy and well-adjusted before entering your home. If you're interested in adding one of these stunning creatures to your collection, be sure to act fast as they are in high demand.

Our chameleons make wonderful pets for those who are willing to put in the time and effort to care for them properly. They require an arboreal habitat with proper lighting, humidity, and temperature levels to thrive. Additionally, a varied diet of insects and supplements is essential for their health and the full expression of their potential. It's important to do thorough research on chameleon care before bringing one home to ensure you can provide them with everything they need to live a happy and healthy life.

When purchasing one of our chameleons, it's also important to consider the commitment involved. Chameleons require regular care and attention. They are not low-maintenance pets and should only be adopted by those who are dedicated to providing them with the best possible care.

Overall, owning a chameleon is a rewarding experience for those who are prepared to meet their unique needs. With their stunning colors and fascinating behavior, chameleons make for a captivating addition to any reptile enthusiast's collection. If you're ready to take on the challenge of caring for one of our incredible panthers, don't hesitate to reach out and start your chameleon journey with us.

Reservation Process

This is a great way to spread out the cost if you need more time to pay down your chameleon.

We run a $75 reservation program for all of our upcoming clutches. Reservations are 100% refundable for any reason and will be applied to your balance when you're ready to ship. You are welcome to pay down more of your balance as you wait for them to hatch and grow.

When the first group of babies reaches the 3 month mark, we run down the list before offering any to the public. You pick your cham in the order you pay, so early reservations get early picks. If you choose to pass or do not respond to our messages within 24 hours, we will post the animals for sale.

We work with customers to get a juvenile from their preferred phenotype. This is not an exact science, but we've observed thousands of baby panther chameleons grow up, and it's your top choice if you're aiming for a particular appearance.

iPardalis (individually-raised pardalis) is an Ambilobe Panther Chameleon breeding company founded in 2015 with the goal of proving that ethical rearing practices of Furcifer pardalis (Panther Chameleons) can be profitable. We are proud to have been there on the ground pushing for this shift in the industry, after listening to Bill Strand’s Chameleon Academy Podcast, and things have come a long way since then.

Why should you individually-raise Panther Chameleons?

Individually-raised Panther Chameleons (Furcifer pardalis) are some of the most brilliant, colorful animals from Madagascar. Panther Chameleons are a good beginning species of chameleon, which can open a whole new chapter in your life with nature. Searching for a reptile with a rainbow hue? You’ve come to the right location.

We specialize in yellow body blue bar (YBBB) Ambilobe Panther Chameleons. Our female YBBB Ambilobe are highly coveted as founding stock animals in the industry due to their vibrant color, robustness, and trustworthy lineage. Despite the lower market value of female panther chameleons often leading to inadequate rearing, we ensure our females receive the same level of care as our hold back males.

We only sell Panther Chameleons that are bred in captivity and are over 12 grams in weight. We do not sell hatchlings, babies, eggs, or wild-caught animals. Every iPardalis Panther Chameleon has a life-long guarantee.

Whatever issue you encounter, even years later, we want to be part of the solution. We talk to customers every day, helping with medical emergencies and answering questions about caring for chameleons and husbandry choices. We pride ourselves on knowing where our chameleons go and helping ensure they live to their fullest potential.

Visit our Facebook or Instagram for regular updates on our project. Our blog covers Panther Chameleon genetics, ethics, photography, podcasts, husbandry and more.

About us

Individually-raised Panther Chameleons. We're a chameleon breeder located in Poolesville, Maryland. We are working with our 7th generation of Ambilobe and 2nd gen of Furcifer minor (Lesser Chameleons). We also have a few colubrid and ball python projects. As lifelong reptile keepers, we hold memberships in Responsible Reptile Keeping and USARK and we sponsor Reptifiles and the Chameleon Forums.
