Huo (火)

January 1, 2020

Beautiful female that we picked up from Kevin Stanford. She has a really nice pitch-black body with a fire tip on the top of her yellow streaks. Stunning specimen of this species! We were able to get a few healthy clutches from her and hope to produce our first clutch from them. She has been very fun to work with thus far.
