Selectively Breeding Furcifer Pardalis

in genetics

October 14, 2022

The best Panther Chameleon breeders have a deep understanding of their genetics, inheritance patterns and can help you get what you want. This knowledge plays into the ethics of selling Panther Chameleons because there is a lot of false advertising in the industry which is very surface-level without much understanding of the species. This is our answer to the filtered images and click-bait on Instagram and TikTok. Introduction Given that Panther Chameleon (Furcifer pardalis) colors are polygenic traits, and Panther Chameleons are a sexually dimorphic species, selective breeding for a given color is challenging and many breeders fail to achieve a consistent result.

About us

Individually-raised Panther Chameleons. We're a chameleon breeder located in Poolesville, Maryland. We are working with our 7th generation of Ambilobe and 2nd gen of Furcifer minor (Lesser Chameleons). We also have a few colubrid and ball python projects. As lifelong reptile keepers, we hold memberships in Responsible Reptile Keeping and USARK and we sponsor Reptifiles and the Chameleon Forums.
