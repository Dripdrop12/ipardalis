Jimanga

December 3, 2021

Jimanga is one of our 5th gen Ambilobe breeders. He comes from our Manga x Imelda pairing, a great collab with Ramblin’ Exotics. I can trace his personality all the way back to Kako. They teach you that chameleons are a look-but-don’t-touch pet, the hard way haha… but what comes with that is an absolutely amazing defense display. Definitely something I really enjoy about these guys!

About us

Individually-raised Panther Chameleons. We're a chameleon breeder located in Poolesville, Maryland. We are working with our 7th generation of Ambilobe and 2nd gen of Furcifer minor (Lesser Chameleons). We also have a few colubrid and ball python projects. As lifelong reptile keepers, we hold memberships in Responsible Reptile Keeping and USARK and we sponsor Reptifiles and the Chameleon Forums.
