Robina

March 7, 2022

Robina is a female we acquired from Ramblin’ Exotics Clapton x Layla pairing. Her sire, Clapton, and dam sire, DynamiteTNT, both have phenomenal blue bars which I believe will complement Ralph’s really well. With any luck, a few Ralph clones could pop out because Robina is an RBBB x RBBB female. She has some interesting turquoise and subtle differences from most of my other females. She is awesome!

About us

Individually-raised Panther Chameleons. We're a chameleon breeder located in Poolesville, Maryland. We are working with our 7th generation of Ambilobe and 2nd gen of Furcifer minor (Lesser Chameleons). We also have a few colubrid and ball python projects. As lifelong reptile keepers, we hold memberships in Responsible Reptile Keeping and USARK and we sponsor Reptifiles and the Chameleon Forums.
